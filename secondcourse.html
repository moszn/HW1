CPSC 4100

Algorithms play a central role in both the science and practice of computing. In this course, the students will learn different algorithm design techniques, including but not limited to, divide-and-conquer, graph traversal, dynamic programming, and greedy techniques. Some important data structures such as binary search tree, red-black tree, order-statistics tree and interval tree will be covered. In addition, analysis of algorithm efficiency, and NP problems will be discussed.

Concepts Learned or Expected to Learn

  • Dynamic Programming
  • Greedy Algorithms
  • Divide and Conquer Algorithms
